Hobies

Snorkelling

One of my passions and hobbies is snorkelling, it is a more expensive hobby and can only be done when my family or friends go on vacation. Snorkeling opens up a vibrant underwater world that’s like exploring a whole new planet. I love floating above coral reefs, watching colorful fish dart around, and feeling connected to the ocean’s rhythm. It’s such a peaceful experience, letting me appreciate the beauty of marine life and the calm beneath the surface.

Playing The Guitar

Playing the guitar is my creative escape, where I get to express myself through music. I enjoy strumming chords, picking up new songs, and experimenting with different styles. It’s satisfying to hear a melody come together under my fingers, and the guitar has a way of helping me unwind and find my own rhythm.

Rugby

Rugby is an adrenaline rush and a test of strength, strategy, and teamwork. I love the fast pace and physical challenge of the game, as well as the camaraderie that comes with it. The intense energy on the field and the shared drive with teammates make rugby a sport that’s as much about connection as it is about competition.
